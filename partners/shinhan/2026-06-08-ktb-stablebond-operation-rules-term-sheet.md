# Shinhan × Etherfuse Stablebond Operation Rules Term Sheet

> **Non-Binding.** This Term Sheet is a guideline for operational discussions and does not create legally binding obligations unless definitive agreements are executed.

**Parties:** Etherfuse MX, S.A. de C.V. ("Etherfuse") and Shinhan Securities ("Shinhan")  
**Asset:** KRW-denominated Korean government bonds (and, as applicable, Monetary Stabilization Bonds and other KRW-denominated sovereign/quasi-sovereign instruments)

---

## 1. Purpose

Procedures and day-to-day operating principles for purchase and sale of KRW-denominated Korean government bonds between Etherfuse and Shinhan Securities.

---

## 2. Key Definitions

| Term | Definition |
|------|-----------|
| **Purchase Request** | Etherfuse's request to Shinhan to purchase bonds meeting specified conditions (notional amount, remaining maturity/duration) |
| **Funds Received** | Etherfuse's international wire credited to Shinhan's designated account and available for use |
| **Executed Amount** | Consideration for bond trades actually executed (including OTC and/or Shinhan internal book) |
| **Valuation Yield** | Benchmark yield calculated and published by Korean bond pricing agencies for the relevant bond |

---

## 3. Weekly Purchase Request Cycle

- Etherfuse submits Purchase Requests to Shinhan every **Wednesday KST**
- Wednesday KST = prior Wednesday PST (KST is ~17 hours ahead of PST)
- Practical rule: **"Wednesday KST = Thursday PT"** — parties confirm exact timing as needed
- During daylight saving time, PT may shift by one hour

---

## 4. Funding, FX Conversion & Credit

1. Etherfuse wires required amount to Shinhan's designated USD account ahead of requested date (accounting for wire transfer lead times and banking cut-offs)
2. Upon Funds Received confirmation, Shinhan executes USD-to-KRW FX conversion as necessary and credits KRW to Etherfuse's Shinhan securities account
3. **Wire instructions:**

   - SWIFT: **SHBKKRSE**

## 5. Market Constraints & Execution Principles

**OTC Bond Market Characteristics:**
- Korean OTC bond market typically trades in **KRW 1.0 billion lots** (or comparable block sizes)
- Short-dated instruments (≤1 year, or <90 days) may be difficult to source depending on supply/demand
- Shinhan uses commercially reasonable efforts to satisfy Etherfuse's terms (notional, maturity)
- If sourcing fails after **10 business days**, Shinhan shares reasons and may re-attempt after ~1-2 weeks
- If strict terms can't be met, Shinhan may propose alternative KTBs within same/similar maturity bucket (subject to Etherfuse approval)

### Immediate Execution Policy
- Etherfuse specifies desired terms in Purchase Request email (notional, remaining maturity, other conditions)
- If Shinhan identifies satisfying quotes, may **execute immediately without reconfirmation** and provide post-trade notification
- Shinhan may limit/defer execution due to market volatility, insufficient liquidity, or compliance/risk limits — in which case shares reasons and alternatives

---

## 6. Price (Yield) Tolerance: ±3 bps

| Rule | Detail |
|------|--------|
| **Basic Principle** | Shinhan may execute within **±3 bps of Valuation Yield** without further confirmation |
| **Rationale** | Reflects OTC liquidity, intraday yield movements, block-lot constraints |
| **Narrowing** | At Etherfuse's request, band may be narrowed (e.g., to ±1 bps). Narrower = higher failure risk + potential idle funds |
| **Outside Band** | Requires Etherfuse's **prior written approval** (email) before execution |

---

## 7. Shinhan Internal Book

- Shinhan may source/execute via its **internal book** (not only OTC market)
- Internal book may offer more favorable terms (spread/yield) but subject to Shinhan's inventory
- Shinhan shall periodically share a **candidate list** of bonds available via internal book (issue/maturity/yield/available size) — frequency/format to be agreed at working level

---

## 8. Partial Execution & Token Minting (Correspondence Principle)

- If only a portion of a Purchase Request is filled (e.g., requested $1M, executed $800K), the Executed Amount is final for that order
- **Etherfuse shall mint Stablebond tokens ONLY up to the Executed Amount** — no pre-minting for unexecuted portions

---

## 9. Settlement

- Settlement: **T+0 to T+1** depending on sourcing route (OTC/internal book/counterparty terms)
- Post-execution, Shinhan shares with Etherfuse: settlement date, settlement amount, applicable FX rate
- **Primary working channel: Email.** Telegram may be used as needed.

---
