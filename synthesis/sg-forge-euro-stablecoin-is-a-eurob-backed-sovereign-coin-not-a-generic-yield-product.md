# SG Forge's EUR stablecoin is a EUROB-backed sovereign coin, not a generic yield product

> The AHA-brokered SG Forge intro is framed as a vague 'yield product' for a ~€12M idle euro stablecoin, but Etherfuse already ships EUROB, so the precise, repeatable play is to back SG Forge's coin with EUROB collateral — the exact MXNe model — turning Societe Generale's digital-asset arm into a marquee EUR reference customer.

AHA's Enzo has offered to introduce Etherfuse to the SG Forge product owner because SG Forge — Societe Generale's digital-asset arm — has a **~€12M TVL euro stablecoin live on Stellar that currently lacks any yield product** [wiki/entities/companies/aha-company.md]. The AHA page describes the opportunity only as a generic 'potential yield product,' which understates what Etherfuse can actually offer.

Etherfuse already ships **EUROB**: a live Stablebond backed by EUR-denominated sovereign debt across 8 countries (Germany and Finland at Aaa down to Italy at Baa3), custodied by Spiko [shared/overview.md, shared/master-knowledge-base.md]. The clean move is not to bolt a yield feature onto SG Forge's coin, but to **back it with EUROB collateral** so it becomes a yield-bearing EUR sovereign coin — the identical structure Etherfuse already runs with MXNe, the peso stablecoin backed by tokenized CETES and co-issued with Brale [shared/partners/2023-12-14-brale-mxne-proposal.md]. This is the same pattern already surfaced for Agant (GBP via GILTS) and All Unity (MXN via MXNe), meaning SG Forge slots into an existing, proven playbook rather than a bespoke build.

Why it matters commercially: SG Forge is not a startup wallet — it is Societe Generale, an institution already deep in tokenization (Broadridge supported SocGen's first US digital-bond issuance) [shared/ecosystems/capital-markets.md]. A EUROB-backed SG Forge coin is both revenue (yield-tier revenue share on the backing assets) and an institutional reference name that de-risks every subsequent EUR/European-bank pitch. It also routes through AHA — Stellar's official bank-integration partner whose roster includes DAB, PayPal, and US Bank [wiki/entities/companies/aha-company.md] — so a single SG Forge win warms an entire pipeline of Stellar-committed banks. The relationship is logged as Nurture; the EUROB-backing framing should push it to an active proposal.

**Caveat:** The intro is still pending from Enzo, and the specific integration terms (who mints, custody of the EUROB leg) are unscoped [wiki/entities/companies/aha-company.md].
