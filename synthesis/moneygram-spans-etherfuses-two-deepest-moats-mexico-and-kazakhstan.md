# MoneyGram independently wants both of Etherfuse's strongest markets

> MoneyGram's intro call shows it wants a near-term Mexico pilot (CETES live) and separately volunteered Kazakhstan/Eurasian expansion — the exact two markets where Etherfuse's moat is deepest, letting one remittance giant ride both corridors instead of the Mexico-only framing the call currently assumes.

MoneyGram's Kian Schreiber call is being scoped as a Mexico pilot because 'virtual accounts are already live' there — but Kian also 'flagged potential Eurasian expansion (Kazakhstan and nearby markets) as an area of interest' [shared/meetings/2026-05-13-moneygram-kian-schreiber.md]. Those two are not random: they are precisely the two jurisdictions where Etherfuse has its hardest-to-replicate position.

- **Mexico:** live CETES with BBVA custody, $500M+ sold, free SPEI off-ramp, and an explicit playbook for being the cheap MXN settlement layer for USD remittance flows [shared/partners/canton-mexico-infrastructure.md][shared/partners/anchorage-one-pager.md].
- **Kazakhstan:** MEKKAM bond imminent, distribution locked via Halyk and Freedom (the country's two largest financial groups, distributing through Etherfuse APIs), and a specifically identified China→Kazakhstan→Mexico remittance corridor that today takes a week over SWIFT for a $100 transfer [shared/partners/freedom-etherfuse-case-study.md][shared/partners/2026-05-21-freedom-bank-aidos-zhumagulov.md].

The non-obvious synthesis: MoneyGram is a remittance company, and Etherfuse's two moats are both *remittance-corridor* moats — US→Mexico ($94B/yr) and the underserved China/Central Asia→Mexico path Freedom already flagged as a pilot candidate [shared/partners/canton-mexico-infrastructure.md][shared/partners/2026-05-21-freedom-bank-aidos-zhumagulov.md]. A single MoneyGram integration that starts in Mexico and extends to the KZT/MEKKAM rail covers both corridors with one partner — and the Kazakhstan leg is something no USD-stablecoin-only competitor can give MoneyGram, because Etherfuse is the only authorized issuer of tokenized KZT sovereign debt [shared/partners/ripple-xrpl-partnership-overview.md].

**Action:** the NDA/Slack-channel paperwork is already the agreed next step [shared/meetings/2026-05-13-moneygram-kian-schreiber.md]; the cheap add is to put the Freedom/Halyk Kazakhstan corridor in front of Kian now, while his Eurasian interest is fresh, rather than letting the relationship narrow to a Mexico-only pilot.
