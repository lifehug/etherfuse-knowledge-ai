# Abound is the pre-built distribution + INR fiat rail for the already-roadmapped India T-bill Stablebond

> Etherfuse already plans to ship INDTB (an INR Indian T-bill Stablebond) in Q3 2026, and Abound simultaneously offers both the distribution (500K NRI users) and the one thing Etherfuse lacks in India — an INR/USDC off-ramp that already clears ~20% of the country's USDC volume.

The Abound page frames the deal as an open-ended 'INR stablecoin or yield-bearing bond product' [shared/meetings/2026-04-27-abound-times-of-india-inr-stablecoin.md], but the product already exists on the roadmap: **INDTB — Treasury Bill, INR, India, Baa3/BBB-, status 2026 Q3** [shared/bond-roadmap.md]. The 'yield-bearing bond product' Dave and Nishkaam aligned on *is* INDTB. Abound is therefore not a speculative new build — it's a ready-made launch partner for a product Etherfuse intends to issue anyway within the same quarter.

The sharper, under-stated point is fiat rails. Etherfuse's platform today supports only **MXN (SPEI) and BRL (PIX)** native fiat ramps [shared/institutional-platform-overview.md], with no India rail anywhere in the stack. Abound brings **500K+ users, UPI rails, and roughly 20% of all USDC off-ramp volume in India** [teams/ceo/docs/inbox/linear/2026-04-18-sales-424-abound-times-of-india-inr-stablecoin.md]. That is exactly the same shape as the gaps Coinbase (EUR/GBP) and 1Money (USD/ACH) were brought in to fill — a live, regulated local off-ramp that converts an issued Stablebond into spendable local currency. Abound supplies the India leg of that pattern for free, as the distribution side of the partnership [shared/meetings/2026-04-27-abound-times-of-india-inr-stablecoin.md].

Integration risk is also lower than a cold partner: Abound is backed by **Circle Ventures** and already operates USDC at scale, so the Circle institutional ramp (zero-cost USDC, already live for Etherfuse) plugs straight in [teams/ceo/docs/inbox/linear/2026-04-18-sales-424-abound-times-of-india-inr-stablecoin.md, shared/master-knowledge-base.md]. The same institutional-stablecoin-stack play Etherfuse packaged for IBT (Circle Mint + custody + compliance) is directly reusable here [shared/partners/2026-05-30-etherfuse-ibt-concept-brief.md].

Decision: treat Abound not as a generic 'India lead' but as the named launch distributor for the Q3 INDTB roadmap item, and sequence INDTB issuance to land with the Abound LOI rather than as a separate engineering track. The $130B India remittance market [shared/meetings/2026-04-27-abound-times-of-india-inr-stablecoin.md] is the demand wedge; Abound is the only piece of it currently in pipeline.
