# Abound's INR offshore issuance is a Bermuda Section-8 notification, not a new structure to research

> Abound and Etherfuse agreed an 'offshore issuance structure to navigate US regulatory restrictions' for the INR stablecoin and Dave's open action item is still to 'research custody + regulatory options' — but Etherfuse's own in-flight Bermuda BMA license already solves this, where a new-currency Stablebond like INR requires only a notification under DABA Section 8.

Abound is a Circle Ventures-backed, US-connected fintech (~20% of India's USDC off-ramp), so its INR stablecoin must be issued offshore to avoid US restrictions — exactly what both parties agreed on the April 27 call [shared/meetings/2026-04-27-abound-times-of-india-inr-stablecoin.md]. The deal is now #4 in the top-15 pipeline at $1.5M TVL / $1.1M annual revenue and sits in Negotiation [shared/bd/pipeline-valuation-snapshot-2026-06.md].

The problem: the action items still list 'Research custody + regulatory options for INR issuance' as open, with Dave separately chasing Shinhan, BBVA, and Halyk for an issuance vehicle [shared/meetings/2026-04-27-abound-times-of-india-inr-stablecoin.md]. That is treating as an open question something Etherfuse has already solved elsewhere. The AirTM JV documents the Bermuda DABA framework in detail: 10+ licensed stablecoin issuers, Etherfuse has already initiated the BMA pre-application, ~3–4 month timeline, and — critically — 'Section 8 exemption: New currency Stablebonds require only a notification to BMA — no re-application' [shared/partners/2026-05-13-airtm-jv-proposal.md]. An INR Stablebond is a new currency by definition, so it lands squarely inside that notification-only path.

This extends the existing Bermuda-as-default-issuance-hub conclusion to a deal that is further along (Negotiation, $1.1M) and currently at risk of choosing a slower, separate offshore vehicle. The decision-useful move: redirect Abound's offshore-structure research toward the Etherfuse Bermuda pre-application already in flight, collapsing a 'research custody' workstream into a notification and shortening time-to-launch on the 2–3 month target both sides set [shared/meetings/2026-04-27-abound-times-of-india-inr-stablecoin.md, shared/partners/2026-05-13-airtm-jv-proposal.md].
