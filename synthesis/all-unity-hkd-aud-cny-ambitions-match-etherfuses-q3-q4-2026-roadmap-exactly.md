# All Unity's next currencies are Etherfuse's next Stablebonds

> All Unity's CEO named Hong Kong Dollar, Australian Dollar, and China as its post-EUR expansion targets — the exact three currencies Etherfuse's authoritative bond roadmap already schedules for Q3–Q4 2026, making All Unity a pre-committed distribution/collateral partner for launches Etherfuse is building regardless.

On the January 2026 discovery call, All Unity's CEO Alex flagged interest in issuing stablecoins for **Hong Kong Dollar, Australian Dollar, and potentially China**, with Etherfuse offering to supply 'all of Asia (via Shinhan), Brazil, Korea' as collateral [wiki/entities/companies/all-unity.md]. Etherfuse's own roadmap independently schedules **AUD (Australia, 2026 Q3), CNY (CHNTDB, 2026 Q3), and HKD (HONGFB, Hong Kong Exchange Fund Bill, 2026 Q4)** [shared/bond-roadmap.md]. The overlap is exact — Etherfuse is building the sovereign-bond collateral leg for the precise currencies All Unity wants to mint under its BaFin/MiCAR license.

This reframes the relationship well beyond the MXN mint the deal currently centers on. All Unity's license 'allows tokenization of any currency globally' and is MiCAR-compliant [wiki/entities/companies/all-unity.md], meaning it can issue a regulated EUR-passportable stablecoin for each currency the moment Etherfuse's Stablebond ships — an application of the exact MXNe (Brale) collateral-wrap model [shared/partners/2023-12-14-brale-mxne-proposal.md] to a whole basket of Asian currencies. Because BaFin approval runs ~3 months per new currency [wiki/entities/companies/all-unity.md], the two roadmaps should be sequenced together: All Unity starts its BaFin filing for HKD/AUD/CNY now so the coin and the underlying Stablebond go live in the same window, rather than discovering the demand only after Etherfuse launches.

The practical play: instead of treating All Unity as a single-currency (MXN) discovery lead, package it as the standing MiCAR-regulated European issuer for Etherfuse's entire 2026 Asia-Pacific roster — collateral supplied by Etherfuse, distribution and EU/EEA regulatory wrapper supplied by All Unity.
