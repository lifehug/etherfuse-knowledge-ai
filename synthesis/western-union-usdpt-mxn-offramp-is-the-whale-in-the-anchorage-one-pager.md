# Western Union's USDPT→MXN off-ramp is the buried whale in the Anchorage relationship

> Anchorage issues USDPT for Western Union, and Etherfuse's own one-pager already offers to be the MXN settlement layer for it — turning the largest US–Mexico remittance player's stablecoin into an Etherfuse off-ramp customer, a far bigger prize than the 'CETES custody' the deal is currently scoped as.

The Anchorage relationship has just delivered a live win — CETES custody on Stellar went public June 15, 2026 [shared/partners/anchorage.md]. But the wiki scopes Anchorage purely as a custody/credibility play [wiki/entities/companies/anchorage.md], missing the much larger revenue lever sitting in Etherfuse's own materials.

The April one-pager explicitly names Anchorage as issuer of **four USD stablecoins** and positions Etherfuse as the MXN settlement layer for *all* of them at 5–20 bps: USA-T (Tether), USDtb (Ethena), **USDPT (Western Union, on Solana)**, and USDGO (OSL) [shared/partners/anchorage-one-pager.md]. The stated flow is: Anchorage USD stablecoin in a Porto wallet → swap to CETES on-chain → SPEI off-ramp → MXN in a Mexican bank account [shared/partners/anchorage-one-pager.md].

The standout is USDPT. Western Union is the incumbent in the US→Mexico remittance corridor, which has historically generated ~$94B/year, with Mexico receiving $61.8B in remittances in 2025 [wiki/markets/latam.md]. If Etherfuse becomes the MXN finality rail for Western Union's own stablecoin, that is a structural, high-volume revenue stream — exactly the 'settlement float' and MXN-settlement thesis the concept pages already articulate [wiki/concepts/mxn-settlement.md] — not a one-off custody listing.

Why this is non-obvious and actionable: the live CETES-on-Stellar custody integration is the *technical precondition* for this flow (a regulated venue where Anchorage clients hold the CETES leg), and it now exists. Yet the SALES-26 record and the Anchorage page treat the deal as 'awaiting business case,' framed around institutions holding CETES for yield [teams/sales/2026-06-19-anchorage-digital.md], with no motion on the stablecoin→MXN settlement side despite Dave already floating 'creating markets between Etherfuse CETES and your US products to create remittance flows' [shared/partners/anchorage.md]. The next move is a targeted USDPT/Western Union settlement pitch, not another custody follow-up.
