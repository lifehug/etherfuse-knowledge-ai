# Halyk Finance is a white-label issuer customer hiding inside a custody partner

> Halyk Finance places ~50% of all bonds issued in Kazakhstan and explicitly asked to tokenize its own issuances through Etherfuse — turning an already-confirmed custody/broker partner into a zero-onboarding-cost white-label revenue line distinct from the government-tokenization TAM.

The confirmed Kazakhstan custody relationship has a second, larger gear that the deal framing treats as a footnote. Halyk Finance is contracted as Etherfuse's KZT custodian/broker (nominal holder of state bonds) [shared/partners/2026-05-21-kazakhstan-partnerships.md], but in the same meeting Halyk revealed it holds **50% market share in Kazakhstan bond issuance** — it places half of all locally-placed quasi-sovereign, state-owned, and large-corporate bonds — and 'active interest in tokenizing their own bond issuances to expand investor access' [shared/partners/2026-05-21-halyk-finance-meeting.md]. Roman Assilbekov (Deputy CEO): *"This second part is really very interesting,"* and he asked for a deck to share with their COO and set up a follow-up [shared/partners/2026-05-21-halyk-finance-meeting.md].

This is materially different from the existing 'government-tokenization-wave-is-white-label-tam' finding, which targets sovereigns. Halyk is a private placement agent that already controls deal flow for half the country's primary market and wants to be **both issuer and distribution channel** for tokenized versions of its placements, with Etherfuse as the pure technology/protocol layer [shared/partners/2026-05-21-halyk-finance-meeting.md]. The pitch writes itself with the precedents the team already used in the room: *"BBVA: started with one Mexican product → asked Etherfuse to tokenize all their assets. Shinhan Securities: same pattern in Korea"* [shared/partners/2026-05-21-halyk-finance-meeting.md] — and indeed BBVA tokenizing-all-assets is the same $100M expansion pattern flagged elsewhere [shared/master-knowledge-base.md].

Why this ranks high on impact × speed × ease: the entity is already partnered, KYB'd as a counterparty, and operationally integrated for KZT Stablebond custody [shared/partners/2026-05-21-halyk-finance-framework.md]; the only open action is sending a company deck to their COO [shared/partners/2026-05-21-halyk-finance-meeting.md]. Each tokenized Halyk placement also flows into the same AIFC SPC / AIX listing rails Etherfuse is standing up, compounding the platform's volume in a market where it has already won regulatory entry.
