# Brale already supports Avalanche — deploy MXNe to unblock the $1M trigger

> MXNe is co-issued with Brale, and Brale's infrastructure already supports Avalanche as a deployment chain, so shipping MXNe on Avalanche is a near-zero-cost configuration step that creates ecosystem demand before the ~$1M foundation commitment threshold is met — breaking the chicken-and-egg stall where Ava Labs waits for demand on a chain Etherfuse hasn't shipped to yet.

## The stall

The Avalanche deal has two parallel tracks — foundation treasury investment and native Stablebond chain deployment — and both are stuck in a holding pattern pending Helio Inaba's risk assessment and Matias's treasury review [wiki/entities/companies/avalanche.md]. The ~$1M commitment is the trigger for chain deployment, but with no Etherfuse presence on Avalanche, there's no ecosystem demand signal to justify the commitment.

## The hidden asset

Brale.xyz, which co-issues MXNe with Etherfuse, already supports **Avalanche** as a deployment chain alongside Solana, Ethereum, Polygon, Celo, Optimism, Base, Arbitrum, and Stellar [shared/partners/2023-12-14-brale-mxne-proposal.md]. MXNe is currently live on Solana, Base, Polygon, and Stellar [shared/master-knowledge-base.md] — but not Avalanche. The gap is not engineering; it's one more chain configuration on infrastructure Brale already operates.

## Why this unblocks the deal

Deploying MXNe on Avalanche through Brale gives Etherfuse immediate ecosystem presence *before* asking the Avalanche Foundation for $1M. Early adoption and DeFi composability on Avalanche — even at small scale — provides the traction signal Helio and Matias need. The Avalanche DeFi ecosystem is exactly the environment where the yield-bearing collateral case study already works: a 7% CETES floor in lending markets vs. 0% for stablecoins [shared/bd/why-stablebonds-vs-direct-bonds.md], and the generic DeFi collateral case study (Case Study 5) describes the use case verbatim [shared/bd/generic-case-studies.md].

## The sequence

1. Deploy MXNe on Avalanche via Brale (near-zero marginal cost — Brale already supports the chain)
2. Seed initial DeFi integrations on Avalanche using MXNe/CETES as yield-bearing collateral
3. Present ecosystem traction to Matt Schmenk as evidence for the risk assessment
4. The foundation treasury allocation ($1M in USTRY at ~3.2% APY [shared/master-knowledge-base.md]) becomes a no-brainer pilot that simultaneously triggers full Stablebond chain deployment

This converts a stalled Negotiation-stage deal into an active deployment pipeline, using a partner relationship (Brale) that the Avalanche wiki page doesn't reference at all.
