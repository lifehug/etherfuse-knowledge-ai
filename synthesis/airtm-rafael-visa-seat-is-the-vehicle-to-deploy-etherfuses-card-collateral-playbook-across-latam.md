# AirTM's incoming JV partnership manager brings the Visa relationships Etherfuse's card-collateral playbook needs

> Etherfuse has a fully-documented Visa local-issuer card collateral model that only needs a Visa-connected local issuer to deploy — and the AirTM JV is hiring exactly that: board advisor Rafael, who has direct PayPal/Visa access, as post-launch partnership manager across a 9M-user LATAM wallet.

Etherfuse's Visa card-collateral playbook lets any local issuer with a Visa BIN convert idle settlement float into yield-bearing local-treasury collateral, with tokens burned to fiat off Visa rails at daily settlement — and it explicitly notes "one issuer integration unlocks card issuance for all of Etherfuse's wallet partners in-market" and is replicable in every market with a tokenized sovereign asset [shared/bd/visa-local-issuer-card-collateral.md]. The gating dependency is a Visa relationship and an issuer.

The AirTM JV supplies both. Board advisor Rafael "has access to PayPal, Visa, and major platforms" and is "being engaged as JV partnership manager post-launch" [wiki/entities/companies/airtm.md], hired specifically to "target joint distribution once launched" [shared/partners/2026-05-29-airtm-etherfuse-sync.md]. AirTM operates across LATAM — including Mexico (integration confirmed live) and Argentina as first deployment targets [wiki/entities/companies/airtm.md] — where Etherfuse already ships CETES (Mexico/SPEI, live) and TESOURO (Brazil/PIX) as the exact local collateral the card model runs on [shared/bd/visa-local-issuer-card-collateral.md].

The overlooked connection: the JV is currently scoped as Stablebond + Yield Trading distribution [wiki/entities/companies/airtm.md], but Rafael's Visa access makes a card-collateral leg a near-free add-on that turns AirTM's 9M wallet balances into card-spendable, yield-earning collateral — a differentiated consumer product no USD-stablecoin competitor can match. Recommendation: brief Rafael on the card-collateral playbook at JV launch and target Mexico (CETES, already live) as the first card pilot inside AirTM's app.
