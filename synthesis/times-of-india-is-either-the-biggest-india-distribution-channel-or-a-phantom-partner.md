# The 'Times of India' half of the deal is either Etherfuse's biggest India distribution channel or a phantom — and no source confirms which

> Every primary source on this deal names only Abound; 'Times of India' — credited on the wiki as a major media group providing brand credibility and distribution reach — appears in no meeting, proposal, or contact record, meaning Etherfuse is either ignoring the single largest consumer-distribution asset in its India pipeline or carrying a partner that isn't actually in the room.

The pipeline ranks this deal #4 and the wiki page leans heavily on Times of India as 'one of India's largest and most recognized media groups' that 'could provide significant distribution reach and brand credibility' [wiki/entities/companies/abound-times-of-india-inr-stablecoin.md] — but that claim is explicitly marked [source needed], and the entity page lists **zero contacts on file**.

The one substantive source, the April 27 kickoff (SALES-424), is a call between Dave, AJ, and **Nishkaam Mehta of Abound (joinabound.com)** — no Times of India representative is present, named, or assigned an action item, and the distribution discussed is entirely Abound's (500K+ users, UPI rails, USDC off-ramp) [shared/meetings/2026-04-27-abound-times-of-india-inr-stablecoin.md]. The pipeline operations config simply pins 'Times of India' with a manual $1.05M revenue override [shared/bd/pipeline-report-operations.md], i.e. the name is curated into the report, not sourced from an engagement.

This matters because the asymmetry is enormous. If Times of India is a real distribution partner (e.g. via Abound), it is by far the largest consumer-reach channel anywhere in Etherfuse's India pipeline — a media group reaching tens of millions, dwarfing Abound's 500K NRI users — and it is being left completely unworked, with no contact and no owner. If it is *not* a real partner, the #4-ranked pipeline deal is overstating its distribution thesis. Either way the next step is cheap and high-value: ask Nishkaam directly whether and how Times of India is involved, and either open the channel or correct the record. Compare to the India remittance-market scale ($130B) the deal is chasing [shared/meetings/2026-04-27-abound-times-of-india-inr-stablecoin.md] — a verified TOI distribution leg would be the difference between a niche NRI play and a mass-market one.
