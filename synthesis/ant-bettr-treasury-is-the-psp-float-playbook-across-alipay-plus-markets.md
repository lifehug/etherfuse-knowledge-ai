# Ant's Bettr Treasury priority is the APAC-float playbook, and Alipay+ is its SE-Asia roadmap

> Ant International's own stated top priority — its Bettr Treasury platform and tokenized deposits — is the exact product Etherfuse already proves in the Hong Kong PSP float case study, and every one of Alipay+'s core SE-Asian currencies is already on Etherfuse's 2026 Stablebond roadmap, so the Money20/20 hook should be float-yield + local supply, not a generic 'yield layer.'

The Ant page records its 'Top Priority' verbatim as 'Expanding Bettr Treasury platform and tokenized deposits' and frames Etherfuse only as a generic '[[concepts/stablebonds]] as yield layer for partner banks and wallets' [wiki/entities/companies/ant-international.md]. That hook badly undersells the fit, because Etherfuse has already shipped a working, quantified version of exactly what Bettr Treasury is trying to build.

**The float product already exists as a case study.** The Hong Kong PSP study shows multi-currency APAC settlement float (PHP, IDR, THB, VND) parked in USTRY at ~4.5% APY during 48–72h batch windows, with corridor FX collapsing from 80–150 bps to 10–22 bps and six correspondent banks replaced by one integration — yield alone (~$135K/yr on $3M float) covering the integration cost [shared/bd/case-study-psp-hongkong.md]. Ant powers 2B+ transactions across emerging markets [wiki/entities/companies/ant-international.md]; its idle settlement float is orders of magnitude larger than the case-study PSP's.

**Alipay+'s markets are already on the roadmap.** Alipay+ runs through wallets in the Philippines, Indonesia, Thailand, Vietnam and Malaysia — and Etherfuse's 2026 pipeline already lists PHP (Q2), MYR (Q3), IDR (Q4) and VND (Q4) Stablebonds [shared/master-knowledge-base.md]. So Ant is not one customer for one yield product; it is a B2B2B2C distribution layer where every new SE-Asian Stablebond can ride into the Alipay+ partner-bank/wallet network the moment it ships.

**Tokenized deposits = Etherfuse's institutional platform, not a competitor.** Ant building 'tokenized deposits' is precisely the full-stack capability Etherfuse already ships — issuance, yield accrual per wallet, multi-chain distribution, proof-of-reserves, compliance and fiat rails out of the box [shared/institutional-platform-overview.md]. The pitch is to supply the yield-bearing sovereign backing and the rails under Ant's deposit token rather than position against it.

**Action:** at the Money20/20 meeting (Tier B, meeting requested, priority/ECV still 'not set' [wiki/entities/companies/ant-international.md]), lead with the PSP float numbers and the PHP/IDR/THB/VND roadmap overlap, and qualify Ant as a treasury-float + local-supply partner — the same under-priced 'event/priority not set' pattern flagged for Airwallex, but with a far larger transaction base behind it.
