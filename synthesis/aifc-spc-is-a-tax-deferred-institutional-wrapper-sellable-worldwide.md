# The AIFC SPC is a tax-deferred institutional wrapper, not just a KZ setup task

> Etherfuse pitched NIC NBK that its offshore vehicle beats the Goldman Sachs route on tax (35% Mexican corporate tax vs. tax-deferred) — and the AIFC SPC's 0% corporate/VAT/withholding regime plus the B+→A credit-rating upgrade turns that into a packaged, globally-sellable institutional product, not a Kazakhstan-only compliance chore.

In the NIC NBK meeting Dave made a cost-comparison argument that has broad reach but is buried as a one-off: institutional clients buying Mexican sovereigns through a Goldman Sachs route get "packaged product, lower yield, corporate tax obligation (35% in Mexico)," whereas the Etherfuse offshore vehicle is an "international investor wrapper, tax-deferred, direct cetes, cheaper" [shared/meetings/2026-05-26-nic-nbk.md]. The AirTM call shows demand for exactly this — Ruben flagged knowing "another token structure... with potentially greater tax efficiency for token holders" [shared/partners/2026-05-29-airtm-etherfuse-sync.md].

The AIFC SPC is the vehicle that delivers it at scale: AIFC-registered entities pay **0% corporate income tax, 0% VAT, 0% withholding tax** [shared/partners/2026-05-25-aifc-talgat-amanbayev.md], and Talgat framed the AIFC structure as the mechanism to move Etherfuse's assets **from a B+ to an A credit rating** — the threshold that unlocks institutional allocators (sovereign wealth funds, family offices, pension allocators) constrained by minimum-rating rules [shared/partners/aifc-gcc-passporting-opportunity.md].

The synthesis no page states: the AIFC SPC is simultaneously (a) a withholding-tax-free, tax-deferred wrapper that beats the bulge-bracket route on net yield, and (b) a credit-rating upgrade that makes Stablebonds eligible for the largest institutional balance sheets. That combination is a *product* — "tax-optimized, A-rated sovereign-yield wrapper" — that should be named and pitched to every institutional prospect (NIC, GCC SWFs, the DTCC collateral track), not treated as a one-time KZ incorporation step at digitalresidence.kz.
