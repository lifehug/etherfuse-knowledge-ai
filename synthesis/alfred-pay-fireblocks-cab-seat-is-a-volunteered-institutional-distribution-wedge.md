# Alfred Pay's Fireblocks CAB seat is volunteered institutional distribution

> Alfred Pay sits on the Fireblocks Customer Advisory Board and offered to champion Etherfuse asset support at the June 2026 CAB event in Amsterdam — a partner-driven, near-zero-cost path to native Stablebond support inside the custody layer that Etherfuse's biggest institutional pitches already assume.

In the April 22 Alfred Pay meeting, Diego disclosed Alfred is on the Fireblocks Customer Advisory Board and committed to **propose adding Etherfuse asset support at the June 2026 Fireblocks CAB event in Amsterdam** [shared/partners/alfred-pay-2026-04-22.md][shared/partners/meetings/alfred-pay-2026-04-22-minutes.md]. This is a won deal acting as a volunteer advocate inside the room where Fireblocks decides which assets to support natively.

Why this is high-leverage and not just a courtesy:

1. **Fireblocks is the custody substrate for the exact institutions Etherfuse is chasing.** Reap — a Hong Kong fintech moving >10% of the global card-linked stablecoin market — runs on Fireblocks [shared/partners/meetings/2026-04-08-reap-research.md]. Native Fireblocks support means every Fireblocks-resident institution can hold and move CETES/USTRY/KTB without a bespoke integration.

2. **It directly satisfies a pitch Etherfuse is already making.** The IBT concept brief and the Shinhan compliance deck both promise 'qualified, MPC/HSM-backed wallet infrastructure' as part of the institutional stablecoin stack [shared/partners/2026-05-30-etherfuse-ibt-concept-brief.md][shared/partners/2026-06-05-shinhan-compliance-presentation.md]. Fireblocks is the canonical MPC custody name — getting Stablebonds whitelisted there turns a promise into a checkbox.

3. **The action item has a hard deadline that has now passed or is imminent.** The June 2026 Amsterdam CAB event was the trigger; the open item 'Re-engage Fireblocks contingent on confirmed flow demand' was gated on Alfred's advocacy [shared/partners/meetings/alfred-pay-2026-04-22-minutes.md]. With Alfred now a Won deal and AirTM/Alfred both generating MXN/BRL stablecoin volume, the 'confirmed flow demand' precondition is already met — this should be activated, not waited on.

The ask is small (asset listing support via an existing customer's advisory voice) and the surface area is enormous (every institution custodying on Fireblocks). Rank: high impact × high speed × very high ease.
