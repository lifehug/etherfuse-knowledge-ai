# Monad Foundation's payments team is an unmanaged emerging-market deal-sourcing channel

> Raj Parekh, Head of Stablecoins & Payments at Monad Foundation, personally brokered the warm intro behind the $1.1M Abound/Times of India deal — meaning Monad is now functioning as a deal-sourcing engine for Etherfuse, not just a chain grant, and should be worked like one.

The entire Abound relationship — a #4 top-15 pipeline deal at $1.5M TVL / $1.1M annual revenue targeting India's $130B remittance market — originated from a single warm email: Raj Parekh, Head of Stablecoins & Payments at Monad Foundation, connected Dave Taylor to Abound founder Nishkaam Mehta [wiki/entities/companies/abound.md, shared/meetings/2026-04-27-abound-times-of-india-inr-stablecoin.md, shared/bd/pipeline-valuation-snapshot-2026-06.md]. The Abound page itself notes this 'suggests Monad Foundation sees value in connecting stablecoin infrastructure players within its ecosystem' [wiki/entities/companies/abound.md].

That framing undersells what is happening. Etherfuse's Monad relationship is normally treated as a grant/integration line — Dave has standing authority to distribute ~$1M in Monad liquidity grants and Monad is one of three networks in the grants workflow [shared/bd/grants-stellar-solana-monad.md]. But Raj Parekh's role — 'Stablecoins & Payments' — gives him cross-portfolio visibility into exactly the kind of local-currency issuers and off-ramps Etherfuse converts best, and he is already acting as a matchmaker. A grant partner who also originates $1M+ deals is a different and more valuable asset.

The move, parallel to treating Brogan Law's referrals as a pipeline, is to make Raj an explicit, recurring intro source: brief him on the live bond roadmap (India INDTB Q3 2026, plus the 50+ country roster) and ask which Monad-ecosystem stablecoin/payment players in those corridors he should introduce next [shared/bond-roadmap.md, wiki/entities/companies/abound.md]. One unmanaged intro already produced a top-15 deal; systematizing the channel is near-zero cost.
