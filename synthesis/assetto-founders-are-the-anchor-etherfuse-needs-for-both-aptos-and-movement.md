# Assetto's founders are the missing use-case anchor for both the stalled Aptos deal and the new Movement deal

> The Assetto team built the #1 DEX and #1 money market on Movement and the #1 money market on Aptos — the exact chains where Etherfuse's own deals are stuck waiting for a credible builder and a 'real use case,' making this one won relationship the warm anchor that unblocks two separate chain-expansion leads.

Assetto (Precept Labs) built **Meridian (the largest DEX on Movement)** and **Echelon (the largest money market on Aptos and Movement)** before pivoting to Solana, and its Phase 2 plan is explicitly an Echelon money-market integration with Etherfuse revenue share [wiki/entities/companies/assetto-precept-labs.md]. Two other leads in this exact neighborhood are stalled precisely because they lack what the Assetto founders already have:

- **Aptos** is in Nurture with no confirmed response since April 2026; the page states the core challenge is 'converting relationship warmth into concrete deployment commitments' and that Etherfuse needs a live RWA use case to give its foundation POCs 'stronger ammunition internally' [wiki/entities/companies/Aptos.md]. The single largest money market on Aptos was built by the Assetto team — that is the concrete deployment story.
- **Movement** is a brand-new Proposal-stage deal (SALES-541) where Etherfuse has set a hard bar: it will only deploy 'when there is a real use case and sufficient liquidity — not on the basis of grants or token incentives alone' [wiki/entities/companies/Movement.md]. Assetto's Echelon (largest MM on Movement) plus Meridian (largest DEX on Movement) *is* that use case, and Assetto's own Phase 2 already routes Stablebonds through Echelon money markets [wiki/entities/companies/assetto-precept-labs.md].

**Non-obvious implication:** Etherfuse is treating Aptos, Movement, and Assetto as three unrelated tickets, but the Assetto founders are the connective tissue — the same team that owns the flagship DeFi venues on both chains and already has a Won relationship with Etherfuse (KYB Engaged, ONB-67 open) [wiki/entities/companies/assetto-precept-labs.md]. The action: use Assetto/Echelon as the named liquidity-and-demand anchor to convert the Movement Proposal and re-open the Aptos Nurture lead, rather than re-prospecting both chains cold.
