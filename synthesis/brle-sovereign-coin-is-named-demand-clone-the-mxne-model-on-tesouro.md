# A TESOURO-backed BRLe is stacked, named demand — clone the MXNe model now

> Multiple partners in this neighborhood are independently asking for a BRL stablecoin, and Etherfuse already has every piece to ship one (TESOURO collateral, live PIX rails, and a Brale model explicitly designed to replicate beyond Mexico) — yet there is no BRLe, leaving the demand to fragment into partner-issued coins.

Etherfuse ships MXNe — a CETES-backed peso sovereign coin via Brale — but has no Brazilian equivalent, despite Brazil being its second-largest live market (TESOURO, ~13% BRL yield, custodied at B3) [shared/bond-roadmap.md][shared/overview.md]. The demand for a BRL coin is now arriving from several directions at once inside this single neighborhood:

- **1Money** explicitly offered to launch a 'MXN or BRL stablecoin via their Bermuda entity, with Stablebonds as collateral and yield distributed via the 1Money network,' modeled on the Agora white-label approach [wiki/entities/companies/1money.md]. Left unaddressed, this becomes another partner standing up its own BRL coin on Etherfuse's collateral rather than distributing an Etherfuse-issued one.
- **Bloque** signed onboarding wanting Etherfuse as their Mexico *and* Brazil on-ramp, with PIX integration, starting immediately [shared/meetings/2026-04-15-bloque-etherfuse-onboarding-kickoff.md].
- **Kaia Foundation** is already allocating into TESOURO as a yield product [shared/meetings/2026-04-29-kaia-foundation-paulo-tesoro.md], and the market intel shows live BRL DeFi credit markets forming around TESOURO (Loopscale × TESOURO × BRZ) [shared/master-knowledge-base.md] — i.e., the BRL-denominated demand is real and growing.

The replication path is already designed and near-zero-cost. The original Brale proposal states the MXNe structure was 'intended to replicate across additional jurisdictions' once proven in Mexico [shared/partners/2023-12-14-brale-mxne-proposal.md], and Brale already supports the chains needed. PIX rails are live in production [shared/institutional-platform-overview.md], so the local fiat leg exists today.

**Decision:** Rather than let 1Money mint its own BRL coin in Bermuda (fragmenting the very collateral Etherfuse holds), stand up BRLe via Brale on TESOURO and offer it as the ready-made distribution asset to 1Money (US-BR corridor), Bloque (Brazil on-ramp), and the BRL DeFi venues — capturing the 75–90% revenue-share tier at scale [shared/partners/2023-12-14-brale-mxne-proposal.md] instead of being relegated to backend collateral supplier. This is the same 'reuse, don't fragment' logic already accepted for the EUR-MXN corridor, applied to a bigger, faster-converting market with demand already on the table.
