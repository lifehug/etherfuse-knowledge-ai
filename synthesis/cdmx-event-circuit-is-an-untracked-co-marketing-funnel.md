# The Mexico City event circuit is an untracked co-marketing funnel

> Accesly is the second ecosystem co-marketing relationship (after Glider) flowing through Etherfuse's Andy/Brandon marketing motion off the back of Mexico City events, yet it's logged as a one-source 'Discovery' with no product, no qualification, and no pipeline owner — meaning a repeatable, near-zero-cost top-of-funnel is being run without commercial follow-through.

Accesly entered Etherfuse's orbit purely through an event — winning a hackathon together at CDMX Stablecoin Week in June 2026 — and the only planned next step is 'co-marketing with Brandon,' with Andy still 'reviewing info' and the company's actual product unknown [wiki/entities/companies/accesly.md]. This is not a one-off: Glider is running essentially the same play, coordinating a joint co-marketing announcement (10 June 2026, 8am UTC) directly with Andy/'andysalza' and requesting Etherfuse's reciprocal post [shared/partners/glider.md]. Two independent partners routing through the same Andy/Brandon co-marketing channel is a motion, not a coincidence.

The Mexico City event surface that feeds it recurs across the neighborhood: the Stellar House Mexico City event (April 21–22) that Dave attended in person [shared/events/2026-04-21-stellar-house-mexico-city.md], and the BMONI onboarding call where Etherfuse used the same Stellar Mexico City event as a relationship-deepening invite into BMONI's Slack [shared/meetings/2026-04-15-bmoni-etherfuse-kyb-business-onboarding.md]. Mexico is Etherfuse's anchor market with the deepest live product (CETES, MXNe, SPEI ramps), so in-person CDMX presence is the cheapest place to convert ecosystem goodwill into integrations.

The gap: co-marketing spend (Brandon's time, brand association) is being committed to Accesly before anyone knows what Accesly does or whether it can become a USTRY/CETES/MXNe customer. The Open Questions on the page literally ask 'What is Accesly's core product?' [wiki/entities/companies/accesly.md]. Decision-useful move: before any co-marketing post ships, qualify the partner against the existing product fit (PSP float-yield, MXNe wrap, KYB+RAMP widget like BMONI [shared/meetings/2026-04-15-bmoni-etherfuse-kyb-business-onboarding.md]) so the marketing asset doubles as a commercial wedge rather than a brand favor with no revenue line. The event circuit itself should be treated as a named pipeline source, since it has already produced at least two co-marketing relationships and reinforced one onboarding deal.
