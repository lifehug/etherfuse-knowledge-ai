# AirTM is booked at $525K revenue but the JV's own math says $600K–$1.2M/yr in yield alone

> The June pipeline snapshot books AirTM at $750K TVL / $525K revenue (#7), but the partnership proposal it sprang from projects $600K–$1.2M/yr in partner yield revenue plus FX on $1.2B annual volume — meaning the deal that just escalated to a co-owned regulated issuer is being prioritized off a number that predates and badly understates it.

The June 2026 valuation snapshot pins AirTM at **$750K TVL / $525K annual revenue**, ranking it #7 in the top 15 [shared/bd/pipeline-valuation-snapshot-2026-06.md]. That figure was a B2B-integration estimate. The relationship has since become a **co-owned, BMA-DABA-licensed issuer (AirTM × Etherfuse BDA Ltd.)** distributing Stablebonds and yield-trading products across LATAM with equal revenue split [wiki/entities/companies/airtm.md].

AirTM's own partnership proposal sizes the opportunity an order of magnitude higher against its real base — **2.5M+ users and $1.2B in annual stablecoin transaction volume** (the entity page cites 9M+ users from the BMA submission) [shared/partners/airtm-partnership-proposal.md] [wiki/entities/companies/airtm.md]. The proposal's Track 2 math: at $200M in assets across products, AirTM earns **$600K–$1.2M/yr in partner yield revenue** — more than the entire pipeline revenue currently booked — *plus* Track 3 float yield of $50–100K/yr on $100M/month in enterprise payouts, *plus* FX margin compression from 3–5% to 3–20 bps [shared/partners/airtm-partnership-proposal.md].

This is the same gap surfaced for Shinhan, where the pipeline number ignored a far larger payment-flow opportunity — but here it's a Won, equity-aligned JV with a 9M-user distribution base. The implication for scheduling leverage: AirTM should be resourced as a top-3 strategic relationship (its own structural ceiling rivals Halyk/BBVA/Shinhan), not a mid-tier $525K line. The LOI was targeted for ~June 12 with **competing proposals confirmed to exist** [shared/partners/2026-05-29-airtm-etherfuse-sync.md] — under-prioritizing it on the stale number is the concrete risk.
