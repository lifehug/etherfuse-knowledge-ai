# Compass Labs is a partner-driven distribution wedge into NIC NBK

> Compass Labs is an in-flight (KYB starting) Kazakhstan RWA aggregator that says it is onboarding 'our national investment committee' and wants Etherfuse's full Stablebond roster plus the coming Kazakh bond — making it a partner-led channel into the exact NIC NBK capital pool that 3iQ and the direct NIC pitch are separately chasing.

Three pages in this neighborhood describe three different routes to the same prize — the National Investment Corporation of the National Bank of Kazakhstan (NIC NBK) — but only one is already moving on its own.

**Route 1 — direct.** Dave pitched NIC NBK on stablebonds, an on-chain index, and pre-deployment USD parking; NIC's bottleneck is custody/regulatory infrastructure on their side, not product fit, and interest was rated 'High' [shared/meetings/2026-05-26-nic-nbk.md].

**Route 2 — fund wrapper.** 3iQ is flagged as a capital/distribution partner from a single Kazakhstan Summit panel, with no formal discussions started [wiki/entities/companies/3iq.md].

**Route 3 — Compass Labs, and it's the live one.** Aidar Pochanov (Kazakhstan-based, UK company) runs a B2B2C RWA/yield/credit aggregator for wallets and neobanks and is explicitly 'becoming like a one place, one stop shop for all the RWAs.' Crucially: *"The reason why I went to Astana basically last week is that we are onboarding like our national investment committee... they're basically interested in everything that we provide, including Yields, credits and RWAs. So we want to cover as much of RWAs as it is possible"* [shared/partners/compass-labs.md]. 'National investment committee' onboarded out of Astana is almost certainly NIC NBK or an adjacent state vehicle — the same institutional buyer Routes 1 and 2 target.

**Why this is the fast lane:** Compass Labs ended the call agreeing to start KYB 'like Friday and next week,' with Andy sending onboarding + sandbox access — i.e., this relationship is already converting while 3iQ sits at zero contact and NIC waits on its own custody build [shared/partners/compass-labs.md][wiki/entities/companies/3iq.md]. Etherfuse's 'one integration, you're gonna have them all' pitch is exactly the multi-RWA coverage Compass needs for its NIC mandate, and the imminent Kazakh MEKKAM bond gives Compass the local product NIC will want most [shared/partners/compass-labs.md][shared/partners/ripple-xrpl-partnership-overview.md].

**Two integration constraints to act on now:** (1) Compass is EVM-only, so the activatable rails are Base/Polygon — Solana/Stellar liquidity is irrelevant to them [shared/partners/compass-labs.md]; (2) their use case is lending/looping, so the Feather/Morpho and Loopscale vault listings are the hook, not just spot. The action: treat Compass not as a generic listing channel but as a named back-door into NIC NBK — coordinate the Compass KYB with the direct NIC pitch so Etherfuse shows up to NIC both directly and as Compass's underlying supply.
