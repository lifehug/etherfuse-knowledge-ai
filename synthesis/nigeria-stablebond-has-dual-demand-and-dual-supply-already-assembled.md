# Nigeria Stablebond has demand from two independent channels and supply from two complementary ramp partners already assembled

> BeKey's users are repeatedly asking for Nigerian yield and BeKey offered to introduce Etherfuse to CNGN (the Nigerian stablecoin), while the Meta questionnaire shows Etherfuse already operates Quidax as its institutional NGN rail — together these give Etherfuse both retail and institutional ramps plus both retail and institutional demand for a Nigerian Stablebond, without a single new partnership required.

Two sources in this neighborhood independently surface Nigeria demand and supply:

**Demand side:**
- *Retail:* In the BeKey call (April 15, 2026), Dave says: *"I keep getting people asking me for Nigerian yield. And so I'm going to look into it."* [shared/partners/bekey.md]
- *Institutional:* The Meta questionnaire (May 2026) describes a $50–75M Nigeria repatriation corridor, already scoped with staged execution at 8–10 bps above Monierate composite mid [shared/partners/meta-questionnaire-response.md]

**Supply side:**
- *Institutional:* Quidax (Nigeria's first SEC-licensed DAX, provisional license August 2024) is already Etherfuse's NGN fiat rail partner, with Fireblocks custody and a $300K+ minimum OTC desk. The partnership has been built "for over a year" [shared/partners/meta-questionnaire-response.md]
- *Retail:* BeKey's Natasha immediately offered to introduce Etherfuse to CNGN (Nigerian stablecoin project, backed by BeKey investor Yuan): *"100%. Both arms and on-ramp and off-ramp, we can lean on us, Dave."* [shared/partners/bekey.md]

**The gap CNGN fills:** Quidax's OTC desk serves institutional scale ($300K+ minimum). CNGN, via BeKey's P2P and ground network, would provide the retail on/off ramp for a Nigerian Stablebond product — the same architecture as the AirTM partnership (P2P ramps in countries without institutional rails) [shared/partners/airtm-partnership-proposal.md]. Dave's action item to connect with CNGN was assigned in the BeKey call but there's no follow-up recorded.

**Competitive context:** Nigeria's peak 24-hour NGN P2P stablecoin market is approximately $48M [shared/partners/meta-questionnaire-response.md], and Etherfuse already takes 100–150 bps on high-yield markets like Nigeria and Turkey [shared/partners/mancio.md]. A Nigerian Stablebond at those margins on even a fraction of daily P2P volume is material revenue.
