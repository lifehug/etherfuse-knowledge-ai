# Agant's GBP stablecoin duplicates Etherfuse's live GILTS — sell the sovereign-coin model, don't call it 'complementary'

> The Agant page frames the relationship as 'complementary rather than competitive' because Agant tokenizes UK Gilts while Etherfuse does MXN/CETES — but Etherfuse already ships GILTS (live, GBP, UK Gilts, Archax custody), so the real play is backing Agant's GBP coin with GILTS collateral (a GBPe), the exact MXNe model.

The Agant page asserts the fit is *"complementary rather than competitive -- they tokenize GBP/UK Gilts while Etherfuse focuses on MXN/CETES"* [wiki/entities/companies/agant.md]. That framing is wrong on the facts: Etherfuse's own roadmap lists **GILTS — GBP — United Kingdom — UK Gilts — Live**, custodied at Archax [shared/bond-roadmap.md] [shared/overview.md]. Agant is building precisely the instrument Etherfuse already issues, which makes the current framing a blind spot that risks funding a competitor to GILTS via grant support.

The correct move mirrors the MXNe / All Unity playbook already applied to other currencies: rather than help a partner stand up a parallel UK-Gilt tokenizer, offer to **back Agant's GBP stablecoin with Etherfuse GILTS collateral** — a GBPe sovereign coin. This is exactly the Brale MXNe structure, which was explicitly designed so that *"once proven in Mexico, the model is intended to replicate across additional jurisdictions"* with revenue-share tiers up to 90% at scale [shared/partners/2023-12-14-brale-mxne-proposal.md]. Agant supplies UK distribution and the GBP-native brand; Etherfuse supplies the collateral, mint/burn, and — critically — the very Bermuda license Agant's own founder helped secure [wiki/entities/companies/bma.md]. The BMA-discussed principal/yield token split is a natural fit: the principal token becomes Agant's GBP stablecoin, GILTS the yield-bearing reserve [wiki/entities/companies/bma.md]. This converts a fuzzy 'grant + cross-promo' Discovery lead into a defined, recurring-revenue sovereign-coin deal without fragmenting Etherfuse's GBP position into a fifth competing coin.
