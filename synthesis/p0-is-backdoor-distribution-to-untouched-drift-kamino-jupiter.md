# P0 is a backdoor into the Drift/Kamino/Jupiter wishlist Etherfuse never contacted

> P0 is a cross-venue portfolio-margin aggregator whose collateral is borrowable on Jupiter, Drift, and Kamino, so a single P0 listing makes Stablebonds usable across those venues without separate integrations — reaching the exact Solana DEXs sitting untouched on Etherfuse's own wishlist.

P0's core mechanic is that users "consolidate positions across venues (Jupiter, Drift, Kamino, etc.) into one unified collateral portfolio and borrow against it cross-venue" [wiki/entities/companies/0xyz.md]. The active P0 deal is to list all 6 Stablebonds as yield-bearing collateral on this layer [wiki/entities/companies/0xyz.md]. That means the moment Stablebonds are accepted P0 collateral, holders can borrow against them across every venue P0 touches — Drift and Kamino included.

Meanwhile **Drift is on Etherfuse's own wishlist with literally zero outreach**: stage "Wishlist," blocker "No outreach initiated," next step "Identify BD contacts at Drift" — open since Oct 2025 [wiki/entities/companies/Drift.md]. The stated Drift opportunity is exactly "listing Stablebonds as collateral on Drift, enabling users to earn government bond yields on their margin while trading" [wiki/entities/companies/Drift.md] — which is precisely what the P0 integration delivers indirectly, across Drift *and* Kamino *and* Jupiter at once.

The leverage compounds because **both deals share one owner**: Andres Salcedo is the commercial assignee on P0 [wiki/entities/companies/0xyz.md] and the assignee on the stalled Drift wishlist item [wiki/entities/companies/Drift.md]. Rather than cold-starting Drift BD, closing the P0 integration (already past Discovery into KYB-engaged-as-priority [wiki/entities/companies/0xyz.md]) is the faster path to the same distribution — and a closed P0 deal becomes the proof point for then approaching Drift/Kamino directly for native listings.

This reframes P0 from "one DeFi lending integration" into a **distribution multiplier** — the same one-integration-reaches-many pattern Etherfuse already recognizes in the Anchorage CETES case, but here aimed at the Solana venue map. Practical implication: P0 should be prioritized not on its own TVL but on the cross-venue reach it unlocks, and the Drift wishlist ticket should be re-scoped as a follow-on to P0 rather than independent cold outreach.
