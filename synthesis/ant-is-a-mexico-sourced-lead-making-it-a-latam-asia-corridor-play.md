# Ant was sourced in Mexico — making it Etherfuse's LATAM↔Asia corridor wedge

> Ant International is a Mexico-originated lead with a Mexico-based primary contact, and its own page names 'LATAM-Asia corridors' as a partnership angle — which is precisely where Etherfuse's unique CETES/MXNe position and its already-proven China→Mexico stablecoin settlement give it something no USD-only competitor can offer Ant.

The Ant lead did not come from an Asia channel — it was sourced from the **FTMX (Fintech Mexico)** attendee list, cold-outreached Feb 23, 2026, with **Alejandro Camacho (alejandro.camacho@ant-intl.com)** as primary contact, and the page explicitly lists 'cross-border payment infrastructure for LATAM-Asia corridors' as an angle [wiki/entities/companies/ant-international.md]. That Mexican origin is the non-obvious lever.

**Etherfuse owns the Mexico leg uniquely.** It is the only CNBV-authorized tokenized-bond issuer, with native SPEI rails, CETES, and the MXNe peso stablecoin co-issued via Brale [shared/master-knowledge-base.md][shared/partners/2023-12-14-brale-mxne-proposal.md]. The remittance case study shows direct USD→MXNe settlement at 5–20 bps with ~7% CETES yield on transit float versus 100–300+ bps on legacy corridors [shared/bd/generic-case-studies.md]. A USD-only stablecoin partner cannot give Ant a yield-bearing, locally-regulated peso leg; Etherfuse can.

**The China→Mexico corridor is already proven.** Etherfuse has separately documented that it 'knows how to move money China → Mexico with stablecoins' as a live pilot candidate for Freedom Bank's WeChat/Alipay-adjacent flows [shared/partners/2026-05-21-freedom-bank-aidos-zhumagulov.md]. Ant operates Alipay+ — the very network on the China side of that corridor. The same route Etherfuse is pitching through Kazakhstan can be pitched directly to its source operator.

**Why it matters for prioritization:** Ant's scale (2B+ transactions, Alipay+ across Asia, Dubai/MENA expansion) makes it a high-ceiling distribution channel [wiki/entities/companies/ant-international.md], and the warmest, most differentiated entry point isn't a generic yield demo in Singapore — it's the Mexico contact already in hand plus the one corridor where Etherfuse has a regulatory and product moat Ant cannot get from Circle or Tether. Engage Alejandro Camacho on the LATAM↔Asia / China→Mexico framing before the deal defaults back to a commoditized 'yield layer' conversation.
