# Agant delivered Etherfuse's entire Bermuda strategy — and is still parked at 'Discovery / no priority'

> The single most valuable output of the Agant relationship is not a GBP deal but the BMA introduction Andrew MacKenzie made, which now underpins the AirTM JV, the Abound INR structure, and the whole whitelabel-issuance thesis — yet Agant is logged as an unprioritized Discovery lead.

The Agant page classifies the relationship as **stage Discovery, priority None**, a revived 2025 lost lead [wiki/entities/companies/agant.md]. But buried in its own Open Questions is the resolution: *"Andrew introduced Dave to Christos Efthymiopoulos at BMA... Bermuda DABA framework confirmed as strong fit"* [wiki/entities/companies/agant.md]. The BMA page confirms Christos — Etherfuse's sole named regulatory contact there — was *"Introduced by Andrew MacKenzie ([[agant]])"* [wiki/entities/companies/bma.md].

That introduction is now load-bearing across the highest-value pipeline. The BMA relationship it opened is what lets Etherfuse pursue a **yield-trading license splitting principal and yield tokens**, replacing the ambiguity of the Mexico no-action letter, with Section 8 notification-only launches for each new currency [wiki/entities/companies/bma.md]. Downstream, the **AirTM JV** — a co-owned BMA-DABA-licensed issuer projecting shared economics on $1.2B annual volume — is structured entirely around this Bermuda vehicle and explicitly notes *"Etherfuse has already initiated the pre-application process with BMA"* [shared/partners/2026-05-13-airtm-jv-proposal.md]. The **Abound INR** deal ($1.1M annual revenue, #4 in the pipeline) agreed an *"offshore issuance structure to navigate US regulatory restrictions"* that the Bermuda license directly answers [shared/meetings/2026-04-27-abound-times-of-india-inr-stablecoin.md] [shared/bd/pipeline-valuation-snapshot-2026-06.md]. AirTM alone is booked at $525K [shared/bd/pipeline-valuation-snapshot-2026-06.md].

A partner who hand-delivered the regulatory keystone for multi-million-dollar deals should not be sitting at zero priority. The near-zero-cost move: upgrade Agant, reciprocate the intro (grant support, GILTS collateral, co-marketing), and lock the relationship before a competitor does — Andrew is an active warm-intro source in the Bermuda/GBP world, exactly the kind of channel the KB already flags as underleveraged elsewhere.
