# BBVA Spark already finances both Etherfuse and Aplazo — a ready warm-intro path

> BBVA Spark expanded Aplazo's credit line to $50M in March 2026, and Etherfuse's own BBVA Spark venture-debt contact (Pablo Soria Garcia) plus its in-flight SETES-collateralized revolving credit line make BBVA the single relationship that can both introduce Aplazo and fund the token issuance an Aplazo-scale rollout would require.

Aplazo and Etherfuse share a financier that no page connects. BBVA Spark expanded Aplazo's credit line to $50M in March 2026 [wiki/entities/companies/aplazo.md], and Etherfuse already has a named BBVA Spark relationship — Pablo Soria Garcia (pablo.soria.garcia@bbva.com), explicitly logged on the venture-debt track [wiki/entities/companies/BBVA.md]. That is a direct, warm intro path into Aplazo's finance org that bypasses the 'enterprise BD is complex' friction and the stalled Bando channel.

The second half of the connection is on the funding side. Etherfuse is structuring with BBVA a **revolving credit line secured by SETES bonds** — drawing credit, pledging SETES, issuing tokens against the drawdown, and repaying as clients purchase tokens — explicitly motivated by 'large institutions expected to onboard within the next few months, driving demand for token issuance' [wiki/entities/companies/BBVA.md]. An Aplazo deployment across 9,000+ merchants is exactly that kind of issuance-demand spike, and BBVA is simultaneously Etherfuse's CETES custodian [wiki/concepts/CETES.md]. The same bank thus sits on three corners at once: Aplazo's lender, Etherfuse's lender/issuance-financier, and the CETES custody backstop.

**Implication:** Etherfuse can use one BBVA relationship to (a) get introduced to Aplazo via BBVA Spark, and (b) finance the token issuance an Aplazo-scale CETES-yield product needs — turning a BNPL discovery lead into a deal where the financing rail is already half-built. Caveat: BBVA's AVE-framework path to its own peso product (noted elsewhere) means the window to position BBVA as a yield partner rather than a competitor is time-sensitive.
