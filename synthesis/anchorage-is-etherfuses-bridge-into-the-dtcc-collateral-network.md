# Anchorage is the shared node that puts Stablebonds inside DTCC's tokenized-collateral consortium

> Anchorage sits inside DTCC's 50-firm tokenization consortium that is moving to Stellar, the same chain Etherfuse already ships on — so an Anchorage custody integration is also the indirect route to making USTRY/GILTS recognized collateral in the largest TradFi collateral-mobility network on earth.

DTCC — custodian of ~$100T in assets — is running an SEC-blessed tokenization pilot for Treasuries/ETFs/equities on Canton, with **commercial launch Oct 2026 and expansion to Stellar in H1 2027**, collaborating with 50+ firms that explicitly include **Anchorage, BlackRock, Goldman Sachs, JPMorgan, and Circle** [shared/partners/2026-06-02-dtcc.md]. DTCC's stated goal is collateral mobility: tokenized assets serving as live on-chain collateral via its 'Collateral Appchain' [shared/partners/2026-06-02-dtcc.md].

Three facts line up that no page connects: (1) Anchorage is already a member of that DTCC consortium [shared/partners/2026-06-02-dtcc.md]; (2) Anchorage provides insured MPC+HSM institutional custody and already supports Stellar, Base, and Polygon — the same chains Etherfuse deploys on [shared/partners/anchorage-digital.md]; and (3) Etherfuse already ships USTRY and GILTS, the exact same asset classes (US Treasuries, UK gilts) DTCC is tokenizing first [shared/partners/2026-06-02-dtcc.md, shared/partners/anchorage-digital-strategy.md].

The non-obvious play: rather than chasing DTCC cold (currently only at Proposal stage [shared/partners/2026-06-02-dtcc.md]), Etherfuse can use Anchorage — a custodian that is simultaneously a near-complete Etherfuse integration partner *and* a DTCC consortium member — as the warm bridge to get Stablebonds recognized as DTC-compatible collateral once DTCC reaches Stellar in 2027. Anchorage custodying Stablebonds is the prerequisite that makes them institution-grade collateral the DTCC network can accept. Confidence is low because the DTCC-Stellar timeline is 2027 and Anchorage's willingness to advocate is unproven, but the chain-and-asset overlap is unusually clean.
