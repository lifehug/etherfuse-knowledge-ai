Dave has already committed to attend Astana Finance Days [shared/partners/2026-05-25-aifc-talgat-amanbayev.md]. Talgat — the AIFC CIO who created the fast-track license — is also personally offering to help navigate Freedom (Turlov is his friend; Freedom sponsors AIFC events) [shared/partners/2026-05-25-aifc-talgat-amanbayev.md].

**The connection no page makes:** a public AIFC Awards recognition of Etherfuse KZ, delivered at AIFC's flagship event by the very institution whose endorsement Freedom is waiting on, is a concrete, cheap instance of the 'public AIFC signal' Freedom set as a precondition — landing right on the edge of Freedom's July–August window. The application costs almost nothing (a form before July 17) and the event attendance is already scheduled.

**Action:** file the AIFC Awards 2026 application before the July 17 deadline and, win or not, use participation/recognition plus the Talgat relationship as the 'AIFC has publicly signaled' proof point in the Freedom July–August follow-up deck.
