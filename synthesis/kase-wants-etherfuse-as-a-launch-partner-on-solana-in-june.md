# KASE's June-2026 Solana digital market explicitly wants Etherfuse as a launch partner

> KASE holds Kazakhstan's digital-asset license #001, is launching a Solana-native (BitGo-custodied) digital market in June 2026, and explicitly welcomed Etherfuse as a launch partner — a faster, more committed listing channel than AIX, and untouched in the pipeline.

The AIX listing opportunity has already been written up, but its sister venue KASE is the more shovel-ready of the two and is nowhere in the pipeline. KASE holds **license #001** from the National Bank for digital-asset operations and is targeting a **June 2026 launch**, built on **Solana** with **BitGo** custody and **Crystal Intelligence** KYC/AML [shared/partners/2026-05-22-kase-digital-assets.md]. Solana is Etherfuse's primary chain and the home of its token-wrap/PT program [shared/entities/companies/Etherfuse.md], so the technical lift to list is minimal versus other venues.

Unlike AIX (no formal engagement initiated [wiki/entities/companies/AIX.md]), KASE has **explicit, mutual interest**: KASE 'wants partners for their new digital market,' said Etherfuse products (liquid short-term KR/BR treasuries) 'would be novel for KZ investors,' and is 'open to any asset if client demand exists' since it can request additions to NBK's allowed-asset list [shared/partners/2026-05-22-kase-digital-assets.md]. Dave's pitch landed (*"If this market is so huge, everybody will find a place under the sun"*), and Etherfuse KZ LLP may qualify as a **direct market participant with no broker license required** if it holds a financial-services license [shared/partners/2026-05-22-kase-digital-assets.md] — a category the AIFC/AFSA fast-track is explicitly built to deliver [shared/partners/2026-05-25-aifc-talgat-amanbayev.md].

KASE's main repo market alone is ~$1T annual turnover [shared/partners/2026-05-22-kase-digital-assets.md], and KASE plans coupon/dividend settlement in stablecoins — a natural future hook for MXNe/Stablebond settlement rails. The open action items are trivial and KASE-owned (send go-live notice, define wallet whitelisting) [shared/partners/2026-05-22-kase-digital-assets.md], making 'be a named launch partner of a national exchange's digital market' one of the easiest, fastest distribution wins available in Central Asia.
