# The Stablecoin Frontiers cohort is an un-worked non-USD reserve pipeline

> The single event that revived Agant put Etherfuse on stage beside Loon Finance (CAD) and inside Lattice Fund's network — each non-USD stablecoin issuer there is a latent Stablebond reserve customer, yet Loon is nowhere in the pipeline and Lattice (thesis-aligned VC) isn't being worked for the customer-led Series B.

Agant's relationship was relit not by sales effort but by a shared speaking slot at Stablecoin Frontiers, where "Agant represented GBP stablecoins alongside Etherfuse (MXN) and Loon Finance (CAD)" — an event organized by Lattice Fund with Bridge as title sponsor, drawing 325+ signups around "non-USD stablecoins, on-chain FX, and agentic payments" [wiki/entities/companies/agant.md]. Two non-obvious things fall out of that line.

First, Loon Finance is a CAD stablecoin builder — structurally the same archetype as Agant: a non-USD issuer that needs a yield-bearing local-currency reserve asset, i.e. a Stablebond. The reserve-backing model is identical regardless of currency [wiki/concepts/stablecoin-collateral.md], and the non-USD stablecoin market is the explicit growth thesis ($1.55B, +260% YoY) [shared/ecosystems/stablecoins.md]. But CAD appears nowhere on the Stablebond roadmap [shared/bond-roadmap.md], and Loon appears nowhere in the June pipeline snapshot [shared/bd/pipeline-valuation-snapshot-2026-06.md] — an un-worked warm lead sitting in plain sight, the same blind-spot pattern flagged for other currencies.

Second, Lattice Fund curated a room full of exactly Etherfuse's target customers (non-USD stablecoin issuers) and is investing against precisely Etherfuse's thesis. Etherfuse is planning a *customer-led* Series B with Shinhan, Stellar, BBVA and Halyk [shared/meetings/2026-05-26-nic-nbk.md]; a thesis-aligned crypto VC that already programs Etherfuse onto its main stage is an obvious candidate to add to that round and a recurring deal-flow source — the same 'the real value is BD introductions, not the headline offering' lesson learned with Monad Foundation, here delivered for free via one event (which already produced the BMA/Bermuda unlock through Agant). The action is cheap: work the Stablecoin Frontiers attendee list as a non-USD-issuer reserve pipeline, and treat Lattice as both an investor and an introducer.
